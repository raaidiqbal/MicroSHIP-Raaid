Column Descriptions

MAL ID: A unique identifier (from MyAnimeList where the database is from) for each anime entry.


Title: The name of the anime.


Score: The average rating of the anime based on user reviews.


Genres: The categories or themes the anime belongs to (e.g., Action, Comedy).


Episodes: The total number of episodes in the anime series.


Air Dates: The start and end dates when the anime was broadcast.


Producers: The companies responsible for funding and managing the anime's production.


Studios: The animation studios that created the anime.


Duration: The length of each episode, typically given in minutes.


Popularity: A ranking based on the number of people who have interacted with the anime (e.g., watched, rated).


Rating: The content rating of the anime (e.g., PG-13, R).


Type: The format of the anime (e.g., TV series, movie, OVA).


Source: The original material the anime is adapted from (e.g., Manga, Light Novel, Original).
